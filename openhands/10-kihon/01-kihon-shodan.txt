Kihon Shodan

Summary: Five punches forward, four blocks back

From yoi:

Chamber fists stacked on right hip and turn R foot out, step L together
Step out L zen kutsu dachi, L chudan kentsui tsuki, R chudan tsuki

Turn L foot out, step R together
Step out R zen kutsu dachi, R chudan kentsui tsuki, L chudan tsuki

Turn R foot out, step L together
Step out L zen kutsu dachi, L chudan kentsui tsuki, R chudan tsuki

Turn L foot out, step R together
Step out R zen kutsu dachi, R chudan kentsui tsuki, L chudan tsuki

Turn R foot out, step L together
Step out L zen kutsu dachi, L chudan kentsui tsuki, R chudan tsuki, kiai.

Rearward movement:

Step back deep L to RFF neko ashi dachi
Chamber L over R on top of R knee, R uchi uke L chambers on hip

Step back deep R to LFF neko ashi dachi
Chamber R over L on top of L knee, L uchi uke R chambers on hip

Step back deep L to RFF neko ashi dachi
Chamber L over R on top of R knee, R uchi uke L chambers on hip

Step back deep R to LFF neko ashi dachi
Chamber R over L on top of L knee, L uchi uke R chambers on hip
